My top 3 movies of all-time.

Doctor G

The Doctor G is a 2022 Indian Hindi-language comedy-drama film directed by Anubhuti Kashyap and produced by Junglee Pictures. It stars Ayushmann Khurrana, Rakul Preet Singh and Shefali Shah. The film follows the struggles of a medical student who is interested in orthopaedics but instead becomes a gynaecologist, and the ensuing chaos. Doctor G was released theatrically on 14 October 2022 and received positive reviews from critics.

Bheed

Bheed is a 2023 Indian Hindi-language black-and-white drama film[3] written, directed and produced by Anubhav Sinha. It is a fictional story set during the events of the 2020 COVID-19 lockdown in India.[4] It stars Rajkummar Rao in the lead role along with Bhumi Pednekar, Dia Mirza, Ashutosh Rana, Pankaj Kapur and Kritika Kamra. Principal photography commenced in October 2021 and ended in December 2021. The film was released on 24 March 2023 to positive reviews, but was a commercial failure.
